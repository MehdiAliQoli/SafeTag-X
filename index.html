Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about SafeTag X

How long does the battery last?

SafeTag X uses a replaceable battery that lasts up to 6 months with normal usage. The app will notify you when battery life is running low.

Is SafeTag X waterproof?

Yes, SafeTag X is water-resistant with an IP67 rating, meaning it can withstand immersion in water up to 1 meter for 30 minutes.

How does the silent SOS feature work?

When activated with a triple tap, SafeTag X silently sends your GPS location, along with an audio recording, to your pre-selected emergency contacts. They'll receive a notification with a map link and continuous location updates.

Does SafeTag X require a subscription?

No, SafeTag X operates on a one-time purchase model. All basic features are included with your purchase. Some advanced features in the app may offer optional premium upgrades in the future.

Can I use SafeTag X without a smartphone?

SafeTag X requires a smartphone with Bluetooth capability and our mobile app to access its full range of features. The basic SOS functionality will still work when in Bluetooth range of your paired phone.
